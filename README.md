ðŸ’¥ Weapon Mechanics
Standard Weapons
Follow parabolic trajectory affected by gravity

Damage decreases with distance from explosion center

Direct hits deal full damage (+$200 bonus)

Special Weapons
Cannon Ball:

Bounces up to 3 times off terrain

Each bounce reduces velocity

Can hit multiple times

Flamethrower:

Creates a cone of fire

Sets targets on fire (4 turns of burning damage)

Melts terrain

Cannot be used while EMP'd

Guided Shot:

Homing projectile

Seeks nearest enemy within 400 units

Turns gradually toward target

Plasma Blast:

Long-range beam

Primary beam damage + explosion at impact

Damages all players near impact point

EMP Blast:

Disables enemy electronics

Prevents special weapon usage for 2 turns

Affected players cannot use flamethrower, plasma, airstrike, guided, or EMP

Also disables movement and shield purchase

Airstrike:

Calls in 5 sequential bombs

Random spread across battlefield

Each bomb has 45 radius and deals 60 damage

Atomic Bomb:

Massive 120 radius explosion

Devastating area damage
